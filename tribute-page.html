Michael Jordan profile

  • 1963 - was born in Brooklyn, New York.
  • 1984 - the Chicago Bulls selected Jordan with the third overall pick of the NBA draft.
  • 1990-91 season - the Bulls beat the Pistons in a four-game sweep in final and won their first championship. Jordan won his second MVP award.
  • 1990-93 - first three-peat of the Chicago.
  • 1993 - Jordan announced his retirement, saying that he had lost his desire to play basketball.
  • 1995 - "I'm back": Jordan return to the NBA.
  • 1995-98 - second three-peat of the Chicago.
  • 1997-98 season - the Last Dance: with Phil Jackson's contract expiring, the pending departures of Scottie Pippen and Dennis Rodman looming, the Bulls won their sixth NBA championship.
  • 2003 - Jordan played in his final NBA game on April 16, 2003, in Philadelphia for Washington Wizards. Jordan received a three-minute standing ovation from his teammates, his opponents, the officials, and the crowd of 21,257 fans.
